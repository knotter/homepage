Basic Elements:

DIV stands for a block level element;

Span represents a inline element.

Now, let's see how it work.

*** DIV 1: Some content here ***
*** DIV 2: Following right after div 1 ***
We can see that two contents are divided into two levels
*** Span 1: some content here *** *** Span 2: following tight after span 1 ***

We can view explicitly the definition of command "div" as well as "span". However, we have another version of

*** DIV 1: Some content here ***
*** DIV 2: Following right after div 1 ***
*** Span 1: some content here *** *** Span 2: following tight after span 1 ***

Apparently, we can use div command without a break to seperate two sentence.

Tips: Div command or Span command is not a child command of each other.
